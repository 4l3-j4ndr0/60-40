LETTER OF SUPPORT FOR VETERANS & FIRST RESPONDERS

Dear Veteran or First Responder,

This message is shared with respect and clarity.

The 60/40 Rescue Veteran & First Responder Mortgage Initiative is a proposed State-level housing protection initiative designed to help eligible Veterans, First Responders, and their families protect their primary residences during periods of financial hardship.

At this time, the Initiative has not yet been adopted. Nothing on this website constitutes an application, approval, or obligation.

Why This Initiative Matters

If adopted by the State of Florida, the 60/40 Initiative would offer:

  • A zero-interest structure on the primary stabilization portion of the mortgage
  • Monthly payments designed to remain within responsible income limits
  • Protection against foreclosure and involuntary displacement
  • Safeguards for surviving spouses and eligible heirs
  • A structured and ethical alternative to foreclosure or forced refinancing

The goal is stability, dignity, and continuity — not debt expansion.

What This Initiative Is — and What It Is Not

This Initiative IS:

  • A housing protection proposal
  • Focused on primary residences
  • Designed exclusively for Veterans and First Responders
  • Structured to prevent foreclosure and displacement

This Initiative IS NOT:

  • A loan application
  • A refinancing offer
  • A replacement for VA or other government benefits
  • A for-profit program
  • A requirement or obligation of any kind

About Expressing Support

Veterans, First Responders, and members of the community may choose to express voluntary support for State consideration of this Initiative.

Expressing support:

  • Does not enroll you in any program
  • Creates no legal or financial obligation
  • Does not affect credit, benefits, or existing mortgages
  • Is used solely to demonstrate community interest

Support expressions help State leaders understand the real needs faced by those who have served.

Important Notice

Only if the State of Florida approves and adopts this Initiative would any future participation be considered — and even then, participation would remain entirely voluntary and subject to eligibility review.

Until such time, this website exists solely for informational and transparency purposes.

With Honor and Respect,
This Initiative is offered in the spirit of service, gratitude, responsibility, and respect for those who have protected our communities and our Nation.
