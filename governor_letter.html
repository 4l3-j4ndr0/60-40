60/40 Rescue Veteran & First Responder
Mortgage Initiative — State of Florida
Date: ___12/24/2025___
LETTER TO THE GOVERNOR OF FLORIDA
The Honorable Ron DeSantis
Governor of the State of Florida
Office of the Governor
400 S. Monroe Street
Tallahassee, FL 32399

I write to respectfully bring to your attention the 60/40 Rescue Veteran & First Responder Mortgage Initiative, a proposed State-level housing protection initiative designed to support Veterans, First Responders, and their families during times of financial hardship.

This Initiative is grounded in a simple and principled objective: to provide a fair, ethical, and sustainable path for individuals who have served our communities to preserve homeownership during periods of economic instability.

The 60/40 Initiative is not a loan product, not a replacement for existing benefits, and not a for-profit program. It is a preventive framework designed to reduce displacement, strengthen communities, and promote long-term housing stability.

The initiative seeks to:

  • Prevent foreclosure and involuntary displacement
  • Support veterans, first responders, and their families
  • Reduce long-term social and economic costs
  • Promote responsible, sustainable housing solutions

Community participation and expressions of support are intended solely to demonstrate public interest. They do not constitute enrollment, confer benefits, or create any legal or financial obligation.

We respectfully submit this initiative for your consideration and welcome the opportunity to provide further clarification or supporting information as needed.

Thank you for your continued service and dedication to the people of Florida.

Respectfully,
60/40 Rescue Veteran & First Responder Mortgage Initiative
Community Information & Public Interest Initiative
